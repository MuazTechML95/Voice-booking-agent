Working Hours
Our technicians are available Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Emergency call-outs may be arranged outside these hours for an additional fee.

Services Offered
Repair Service, Installation, Maintenance, and Inspection for home and office equipment (electrical, plumbing, and appliance services).

Pricing
Inspection Visit: PKR 1,000 (waived if you proceed with the repair). Repair Service: starting at PKR 1,500 depending on the issue. Installation: quoted after a site visit. Annual Maintenance Contracts are available at discounted rates.

Cancellation Policy
Please cancel or reschedule at least 4 hours before your scheduled visit.

Service Area
We currently serve all major areas within the city. Travel charges may apply for locations outside city limits.

Warranty
All repairs and installations come with a 30-day service warranty.
