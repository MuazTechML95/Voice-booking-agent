Working Hours
We are open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Services Offered
We offer General Checkups, Follow-up Visits, New Patient Registration, and Specialist Consultations (Cardiology, Dermatology, Pediatrics, and General Medicine).

Pricing
General Checkup: PKR 1,500. Follow-up Visit: PKR 1,000. Specialist Consultation: PKR 2,500 - 4,000 depending on the specialist. New Patient Registration is free.

Cancellation Policy
Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Insurance
We accept most major health insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment.

Location and Parking
The clinic is located on the ground floor with wheelchair access. Free parking is available in the building's basement.

Emergency Cases
For medical emergencies, please call our emergency line directly instead of booking a regular appointment, or visit the nearest emergency room.
