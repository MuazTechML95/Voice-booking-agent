Working Hours
Our admissions office is open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Services Offered
Admission Counseling, Course Consultation, and Parent-Teacher Meetings for students across all grade levels and several diploma/certificate programs.

Pricing
Admission Counseling: Free. Course Consultation: Free for prospective students. Application processing fee: PKR 2,000 (non-refundable).

Cancellation Policy
Meetings can be rescheduled at no charge with at least 24 hours' notice.

Scholarships
Merit-based and need-based scholarships are available; ask our admissions counselor for eligibility details during your consultation.

Required Documents
Please bring previous academic records and a valid ID card to your admission counseling session.
